Ryomen Sukuna

Ryomen Sukuna, the King of Curses, is the main antagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen. As of late, Sukuna has been defeating the various characters of the series one by one in the manga. The fandom is wondering just how exactly the King of Curses could possibly be defeated, now that most of the cast has been taken out. In fact, many say that Gege Akutami, the author of the series, is making things up has he goes. I made this website to come up with five different ways that the current cast could defeat Sukuna using cunning, wit and determination.

Plan Number 5: Todo Butthole Plan

The first plan to defeat Sukuna involves Todo. Todo's cursed technique allows him to switch the place of two objects with cursed energy. This ability is activated with the clap of his hands. Now it's true that his hands got cut off near the end of Season 2, but there's an easy solution to this. With training and lots of squats, Todo won't need his hands to clap at all.

Todo's way of defeating Sukuna involves a couple of things:

  • An extremely large object imbued with cursed energy, like a cursed semi-truck
  • An extremely small, thin object imbued with cursed energy, like a crayon
  • An opening to shove the small object inside of Sukuna

Todo won't have very many oppurtunities to get a small object inside of Sukuna. Sukuna can grow mouths on his body so shoving it down his mouth won't work. He could conceivably drug Sukuna's food, but we don't yet know if he needs to eat, as we've only ever seen him eat one of his fingers in the series. The only option I see as plausible is if Todo gets it in through another orifice. Then all he has to do is clap and the crayon will switch with the semi-truck and Sukuna will explode. Then they just have to put all his body parts into little containers to prevent him from regenerating.

Plan Number 4: Fortnite

As we all know, in the current manga, Gojo died. He was really the only one who had a real chance of beating Sukuna one-on-one. What so many people seem to forget is that he is still alive- and he's cranking 90s and getting epic victory royales in Fortnite.

As we all know and agree, Fortnite is canon and central to every series it has a crossover with. This means that Gojo is still alive and once he's finished training against Omniman and Peter Griffin, it's only a matter of time before he finds his way back to the JJKverse, most likely the same way he left. Once there, he need only team up with literally every other character in the series. He can even bring with characters from Fortnite to help.

Plan Number 3: Goku

Even if Gojo doesn't bring people from Fortnite, there's someone strong enough to come on his own- Son Goku, the Super Saiyan.

Goku is self-explanatory. Even at his most powerful, Sukuna cannot stand against Goku. I don't really interact with the DBZ fandom all that much because all they do is be aggressively straight at you and powerscale Goku against everything. That means Goku has to be at least as strong as like, a million lions. That's a lot of lions. Sukuna has never even met a lion before. An honorable mention goes out to these characters, who I also feel could easily defeat the King of Curses.

Plan Number 2: Drugs

Sukuna has shown resistance to all kind of attacks and damage. He can shake off blows, heal any injury, but there's one thing he hasn't been confirmed to be resistant to- overwhelming amounts of drugs and poison.

Yuji and the others are fighting Sukuna in Shibuya, in Tokyo. Tokyo is a large urban area, one of the largest in the world. This means the chances of one of the characters fighting Sukuna finding some illegal drugs is actually the HIGHEST out of all the places they could fight him. This means its perfectly plausible for Yuji or Maki to find some crack or something and debuff Sukuna with it. This will even the playing field, and it might even be enough for it to take him out by itself. Do not take drugs unless it is to defeat Sukuna!!!

Plan Number 1: The Miracle of Childbirth

Sukuna is a powerful foe, and his evil knows no bounds- but he could not possibly fight the other sorcerers of Shibuya if he had to worry about his child at the same time.

Given Sukuna's evil feats, it's likely he would be evil enough to sacrifice his unborn child in order to win a fight. The crux of this argument then lies in the debuff to his strength and cursed energy output that comes from being pregnant.
